Crème brûlée

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 vanilla bean (split and scraped)
  • 5 large egg yolks
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons superfine sugar (for topping)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. Heat the cream with the vanilla bean until warm, then remove from heat and let cool.
  3. Whisk egg yolks and sugar together, then gradually mix in the cream.
  4. Pour the mixture into ramekins and bake in a water bath for 40–50 minutes.
  5. Chill the custards, then sprinkle sugar on top and caramelize with a torch before serving.
