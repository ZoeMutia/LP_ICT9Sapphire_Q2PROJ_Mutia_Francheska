Classic Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  • 3 (8 oz) packages cream cheese (softened)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (163°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter. Stir until well mixed.
  3. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan to form the crust. Bake for 10 minutes, then remove from the oven to cool.
  4. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese with sugar and vanilla extract until smooth and creamy.
  5. Add the eggs one at a time, beating after each addition. Mix in the sour cream and heavy cream until smooth.
  6. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the cooled crust and spread evenly.
  7. Bake for 55-60 minutes, or until the center is set but slightly wobbly. Turn off the oven and let the cheesecake cool in the oven with the door slightly ajar for 1 hour.
  8. After cooling, remove the cheesecake from the oven and let it cool completely at room temperature. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight for best results.
  9. Once chilled, remove the cheesecake from the springform pan. Serve as is or with fruit, chocolate drizzle, or whipped cream as desired.
