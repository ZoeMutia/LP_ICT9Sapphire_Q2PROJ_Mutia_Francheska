Classic Bread Pudding

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups of cubed stale bread (preferably brioche or challah)
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup raisins (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter (for greasing the baking dish)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish with butter.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the milk, cream, sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until well combined.
  3. Fold in the cubed bread, making sure all pieces are soaked in the liquid mixture. Let the bread sit for about 10 minutes to absorb the mixture.
  4. If using raisins, fold them into the bread mixture.
  5. Transfer the soaked bread mixture into the prepared baking dish. Spread it out evenly.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.
  7. Remove the bread pudding from the oven and let it cool slightly before serving. Optionally, drizzle with caramel sauce or serve with whipped cream or ice cream.
